  • Why is it vital to keep water out of chimney flues?

    • Rain can dissolve soot, making a caustic solution which reacts with the mortar between the bricks causing loose bricks and mortar gaps giving an exit for fumes or sparks potentially causing fire in roof cavities.


  • Why should I be sweeping my chimney regularly?

    • Keep operation safe by clearing soot and creosote which if allowed to build chokes flue and becomes highly combustible.
